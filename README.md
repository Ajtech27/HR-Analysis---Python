 ## PeopleFirst HR Salary & Workforce Analysis - Python
**Domain:** HR Analytics

A full HR salary and workforce analysis for a fictional company — PeopleFirst Analytics — with 1,000 employees across 8 departments. Structured as a professional report with plain-English insights after every chart.

**11 Analysis Sections:**
- Data overview and quality check
- Overall salary distribution with histogram, KDE curve, and salary band breakdown
- Gender pay gap analysis — company-wide and department-level
- Department salary benchmarking vs company median
- Tenure and education vs salary correlation
- Performance rating vs compensation analysis
- Attrition risk by department, performance, tenure band, and age band
- Workforce demographics — age, gender, and education breakdown
- Executive dashboard — 8 KPI cards in dark theme
- Key findings table and top 5 recommendations

**Key findings:**
 - 9% gender pay gap widest in Sales and Operations
 - New hire attrition nearly double company average
 - Salary compression detected among 8 to 12 year tenure employees
 - Operations median salary 18% below company benchmark

**Tools:** Python · Pandas · NumPy · Matplotlib · Seaborn · SciPy · Jupyter Notebook
